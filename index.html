Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Medical Home (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users. The app does not require or store your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information.

3. Purpose of the App

Medical Home is a healthcare support application that provides users with information about medical discounts and services offered through partnered healthcare providers. The app acts as a platform to present valid health card offers, clinic details, and available discounts.

4. No Account or Login Required

You can use all features of Medical Home without creating an account or logging in. We respect your right to privacy and provide full access to our services without asking for your personal identity.

5. Data Security

As no personal data is collected, there is no risk of data breach related to users' identity. However, we still apply standard security measures to ensure the safe delivery of service and protection of any non-personal technical data that may be used for internal improvement.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not use or integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any user data (personal or anonymous) with any third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

Medical Home is safe for all age groups. Since we do not collect any data, there is no special handling required for children under 13 in compliance with COPPA or similar regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.
